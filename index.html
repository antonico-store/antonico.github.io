How it works

D‑A‑D: Design → Assemble → Delivery

1) Design

Share your photos via WhatsApp. We crop, remove background, and mock up your layered composition for approval.

2) Assemble

We hand‑cut, mount, and finish the frame. Materials are checked for colour and fit before sealing.

3) Delivery

We pack with corner protectors and rigid boxes. Ship via your preferred option or local pickup.

Note: turnaround depends on design complexity and approval speed.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do we start?

Tap Start your design below, share your photo(s), and tell us size + frame type. We’ll send a mockup for approval.

Can I request revisions?

Yes — the first round is included. Extra design revisions or urgent requests can be added if needed.

What about delivery?

We ship across Hong Kong. For SF Express pickup or door‑to‑door, we’ll share options and confirm costs before payment.

Do you keep my photos?

We store work files securely for 30 days to support revisions or reprints, then archive or delete by request.
